# Simple Tomato Sauce

A quick and easy sauce for weeknights.

## Ingredients
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Fresh basil (optional)

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant (about 1 min).
3. Pour in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and let it bubble gently for 15-20 minutes.
5. Stir in fresh basil just before serving.
