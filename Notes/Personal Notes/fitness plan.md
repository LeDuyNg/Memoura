# Weekly Workout Plan

- **Monday:** Chest & Triceps
  - Bench Press: 3x5
  - Incline DB Press: 3x8-10
  - Tricep Dips: 3x12
- **Tuesday:** Back & Biceps
  - Pull-ups: 3 x max reps
  - Barbell Row: 3x5
  - Bicep Curls: 3x10
- **Wednesday:** Rest or light cardio
- **Thursday:** Legs
  - Squats: 3x5
  - Deadlift: 1x5
  - Leg Press: 3x10
- **Friday:** Shoulders & Abs
  - Overhead Press: 3x5
  - Lateral Raises: 3x12
  - Planks: 3 x 60s
